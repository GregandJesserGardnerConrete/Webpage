Our Values and Commitment

Greg & Jesse Gardner Concrete Contractors is bonded, insured and licensed for your peace of mind. We accept bank checks, cash, insurance and personal checks. We have been in business since 1946 and Jesse and Evan are the third and fourth generation to provide quality concrete contracting to homes and businesses in the Twin Cities. Jesse has more than 40 years of experience and has worked hundreds of jobs always providing a professional service. From our base in Minneapolis MN, we serve residential property owners in and around the Metro Area.

Whether you need patios, driveways, walkways, foundations, masonry, paving, or steps, we will do the job right. If you are interested in what we can offer you, please feel free to contact us directly. A member of our experienced team will be delighted to take your call. We look forward to hearing from you.

Our Legacy

Greg and Jesse Gardner Concrete Contractors was founded with a commitment to delivering superior concrete work and outstanding customer service. For over 75 years, we have been a trusted name in the Twin Cities metropolitan area, building our reputation through dedication to quality, reliability, and integrity.

What started as a small family operation has grown into a respected concrete contracting company, but our core values have remained unchanged. We believe in treating every project—no matter the size—with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail. This commitment to excellence has made us the preferred choice for homeowners, contractors, and commercial property owners throughout the region.

Our success is built on the foundation of hard work, skilled craftsmanship, and the trust our customers place in us. With decades of combined experience, our team understands the unique challenges and opportunities of concrete work in Minnesota's climate. We take pride in delivering results that not only meet but exceed expectations.

Father and son team, Jesse and Evan Gardner, have learned the value of our customer satisfaction from a lifetime of experience providing expert concrete installation and repairs. That's why we have made it our mission to provide you with reliable crews, quality workmanship and attention to even the smallest details. Even though our company continues to grow, our belief in the value of an honest, one-on-one relationship remains the same.

Greg and Jesse Gardner Concrete Contractors stand by all that we offer and are proud of the quality of service we provide. We guarantee that by the time our work for you is complete, you will be completely satisfied whether we have laid a garage foundation, smoothed out a walkway or installed a gorgeous patio and steps out of colored or decorative concrete.
