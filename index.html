Welcome to My Journey

I'm Evan Gardner, an Entrepreneurship major at the University of Minnesota Duluth who practices skills and strengths in both sports and academics to live a life that's both exhilarating and successful. I spend my summers mastering my skills in concrete under my father's family business (Greg and Jesse Gardner Concrete) and my winters harnessing my core skills in business while mastering my hobbies. Before enrolling in the university, I went to Richfield High School where I graduated at the top 1% of my class while participating in Baseball, Football, and Volleyball. Since high school I've been dedicated to excelling both physically and mentally to broadening my career paths in the future. I plan to start my own business and/or takeover the family business after graduating at UMD.

Snowboarding

"There's no such thing as too much snow." Snowboarding has been the seasonal hobby of my dreams since I was 9. Whenever someone asks, "What's your favorite season?" there's an obvious reason I pick winter. Snowboarding is the center of thrill and exploration. True beauty comes from the tracks you leave on mountains with fresh snow.

Volleyball
