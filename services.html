Comprehensive Concrete Solutions

At Greg and Jesse Gardner Concrete Contractors, we offer a full range of professional concrete services to meet all your residential and commercial needs. With over 75 years of experience serving the Twin Cities metro area, our skilled team is ready to handle projects of any size and complexity.

From decorative stamped concrete to structural foundations, driveways to repairs, we have the expertise and dedication to deliver exceptional results. Click on any service below to learn more about what we offer and see examples of our work.

Our Services

Concrete Steps

We design and construct durable concrete steps that provide safe, reliable access to your home or building. Our steps are built with precision and attention to detail, featuring proper grading for water drainage and weather resistance. We can create custom designs that match your architectural style, from simple straight steps to curved and decorative options. Whether you need front porch steps, basement stairs, or deck access, we ensure every step is structurally sound and built to last.

Typical Thickness: 5 - 7"

Driveways

We specialize in creating strong, attractive driveways that enhance your property's curb appeal while providing years of reliable service. Our driveways are built with proper subbase preparation and compaction, ensuring excellent drainage and longevity. We offer standard gray concrete as well as decorative options including colored concrete, stamped patterns, and custom finishes. Each driveway is sealed with our triple-action sealer for maximum protection against Minnesota weather.

Typical Thickness: 4 - 5" or greater upon request

Patios

Transform your outdoor space with a beautiful concrete patio from Greg and Jesse Gardner. Whether you want a simple, clean look or an elaborate stamped and colored design, we can create the perfect setting for entertaining and relaxation. Our patios are carefully graded for proper drainage and finished with professional techniques that ensure a smooth, long-lasting surface. We can incorporate decorative elements, custom colors, and unique textures to match your home's style and your personal preferences.

Typical Thickness: 3½ - 4"

Sidewalks

We create reliable, safe sidewalks for residential properties, commercial spaces, and public areas. Our sidewalks are built to withstand heavy foot traffic and Minnesota's challenging weather conditions. We pay careful attention to proper slope for drainage, smooth finishing for safety, and reinforcement for durability. Whether you need a simple utilitarian walkway or a decorative stylized surface, we deliver professional-quality results that enhance accessibility and safety.

Typical Thickness: 3½ - 4"

Garage Slabs

Our garage slabs are engineered to support the weight of vehicles and resist the elements for decades. We use proper subbase preparation with mechanical compaction and reinforcement to prevent cracking and settling. Whether you're building a new garage or replacing an existing slab, we ensure your floor is level, durable, and ready for the demands of daily use. We can also install slabs for additions, work spaces, and other structures requiring a solid concrete foundation.

Typical Thickness: 4 - 5" or greater upon request

Footings

Proper footings are essential for the stability and longevity of any structure. We design and construct footings that meet all building codes and handle Minnesota's freeze-thaw cycles. Our team carefully excavates to proper depth, installs reinforcement, and pours concrete footings that will support decks, additions, fences, and permanent structures for decades. Post footings require special attention to depth and reinforcement, which we provide with precision and expertise.

Typical Thickness: 8" or greater | Post Footings: 42" minimum or greater

Concrete Walls & Retaining Walls

We construct strong, attractive concrete and block walls for a variety of applications. Our retaining walls are engineered to hold back soil while providing an attractive landscape feature. We also offer basement wall repair and new block foundation construction for additions. Each wall project is designed with proper drainage, reinforcement, and structural integrity to ensure years of reliable performance. Whether you need functional retaining walls or decorative block work, our expertise ensures quality results.

We also provide parging for interior or exterior walls to repair scaling foundation walls.

Decorative Concrete

Transform ordinary concrete into a stunning design feature with our decorative concrete services. We specialize in stamped concrete that replicates the appearance of brick, stone, tile, and wood. Our colored concrete options allow you to customize your surface with virtually any color palette. We also offer exposed aggregate finishes, epoxy coatings, and thin concrete overlays to refresh existing concrete. Each decorative project is handled with artistic vision and technical expertise to create beautiful, long-lasting surfaces that enhance your property's value and appeal.

We use top-quality sealers to protect and enhance decorative finishes, ensuring they remain vibrant and protected for years to come.

Core Fills

Core fills are essential for properly supporting block foundation walls and preventing water infiltration. We inject concrete into the hollow cores of concrete block to provide structural reinforcement and create a moisture barrier. This specialized service is critical for basement foundations, especially in Minnesota where freeze-thaw cycles and moisture are constant challenges. Our core fill work is done with precision to ensure complete filling and proper curing for maximum protection and longevity.

Core fills also help prevent pest entry and improve the overall integrity of your foundation walls.

Concrete Repairs

From minor cracks to major structural issues, we provide expert concrete repair services to extend the life of your concrete surfaces. We assess damage thoroughly and recommend the most effective repair approach. Common repairs include crack filling, spalling repair, concrete patching, and joint repair. We use quality repair materials and proven techniques to ensure repairs are durable and blend seamlessly with existing concrete. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can prevent small issues from becoming expensive problems.

We also provide window well installation, drain tile repair, and exterior grading corrections around foundations.

Concrete Coloring & Staining

Add personality and style to your concrete surfaces with professional coloring and staining services. We offer integral coloring that's mixed into the concrete during placement for consistent, long-lasting color. We also provide acid staining and acrylic stains that create unique, natural-looking effects with depth and character. Whether you want a subtle earth tone or a bold custom color, our team can create the exact look you envision. Colored concrete adds visual interest while maintaining the durability concrete is known for.

Colored concrete works beautifully with stamped finishes for truly unique decorative projects.

Power Washing & Resealing

Keep your concrete looking new with professional power washing and resealing services. Over time, concrete accumulates dirt, mold, algae, and weathering that dull its appearance. Our power washing removes this buildup safely and effectively, revealing the original beauty of your concrete. Following cleaning, we apply our triple-action sealer to protect against future damage, UV fading, and moisture infiltration. Regular resealing every 2-3 years significantly extends the life of your concrete investment and keeps surfaces looking fresh.

We use professional-grade equipment and proven techniques to clean driveways, patios, sidewalks, and paver surfaces.
